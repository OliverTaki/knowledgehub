Operating model

Wire is a first-pass capture lane. Articles and Library are built through follow-up review.

  • Wire

    Original links and public summaries remain visible for later judgement.

  • Articles

    Synthesis notes and judged writing are separated from raw capture.

  • Library

    Durable books, films, tools, workflows, tutorials, and references are kept in a flat shelf.

The site style follows Autograph Hub. The topic model remains specific to Knowledge Hub.
